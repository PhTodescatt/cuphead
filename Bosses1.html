Curiosities

Naturally, the first island has simpler bosses, but they already show the high level of game difficulty

The first boss is the Root Pack, where we have a Potato that throws balls, a crying Onion and finally a Carrot that shoots lightning and attacks us with other smaller carrots. Being an extremely simple, to adapt to the game

A blue ball, Goopy Le Grande. In the first phase and second phase he attacks us with jumps, changing just in size and adding a punch in the second half. Finally, he becomes a tombstone, in attacking from side to side

The Ribby and Croaks frogs are more complex, alternating between bee attacks and flying gloves in first phase, and with one of them becoming a fan in the second. In the last part, they come together and become a machine casino, having 3 varied attacks

Hilda Berg is the first aerial boss we face, having several attacks. During all phases she sends small ships that shoot at our character, while it turns into constellation characters during battle, becoming a Bull, a Couple and an Archer. Finally, it turns into a giant moon and ships and stars harm us

The Cagney Carnation flower has few attacks, but each one comes with several small setbacks, such as seeds and carnivorous plants, which attack us as she throws more and more attacks. She also becomes a flower with an even larger and thorny root
