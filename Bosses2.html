Curiosities

Baroness Von Bon Bon is a boss with 5 mini bosses before the final battle against her, being a Gumball floating, a flying Waffle that divides into several parts, a flying Corn Candy, a Cupcake that jumps and a running gumball machine. All this raffled off in just 3 of them that will appear before of the Baroness, the main chief

A Genie called Jimmy The Great, with different types of attacks and taking on different forms, becoming some pillars, a sarcophagus, summoning a puppet and at the end becoming huge and attacking us with pyramids

Beppi the Clown, our battle takes place in an amusement park. it also takes different forms, first being in a cart, then it turns into a balloon, rides a toy horse and finally turns into a giant carousel, all while a train passes through the scene trying to attack us

Wally Warbles, a giant woodpecker that shoots eggs and ammunition in the first phase, plays think in the second, a baby bird appears in the third and he tries to beat us with his last strength carried on a stretcher in the final stage

We also face a dragon, commonly classified as one of the most difficult in the game. Grim Matchstick starts throwing fireballs and laser attacks at our character, then releases small balls of fluff with life that jump at us, and finally turns into a three-headed dragon with a fire attack in directions random
