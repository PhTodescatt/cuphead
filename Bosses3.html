Curiosities

The first boss of Island 3, Rumor Honeybottoms has a very interesting concept: the setting is a hive with several small platforms, and this scenario is rising and we must follow this rise to avoid suffering damage of the honey that lies just below. All this while we deal with a guard bee in the first phase, the bee queen appears in the second with 3 different attacks and at the end she turns into a plane that shoots at Cuphead

The mouse and the cat, known for appearing to be inspired by Tom and Jerry. Werner Werman has simple attacks, as a mouse he throws tomatoes and debris from a can, while old bottle caps are thrown at the attack, and while the cat tries to attack us with its paw and with ghosts

The boss classified as the most difficult in the game by the community has different types of attacks. Dr. Kahl's Robot begins with 3 attacks, through the robot's belly, antenna and chest; then it just turns its head, flying to everyone the directions as bombs chase us; at the end the Doctor appears in the Robot's head, being a phase with very high health and attacks several diamond fragments while we dodge

The Mermaid Cala Maria has 3 types of attack in her first phase, while other characters play water or bombs on the player, and all these attacks can come at the same time; in the second phase it becomes a species of jellyfish and the snakes in her hair attack us; Finally, her body becomes a statue and her head starts attacking us in a tunnel

Pirate Captain Brineybeard and his Ship are very simple, at the beginning the boss's hitbox is concentrated all over the pirate's body, while attacking with different types of characters that emerge from the water; in the second and final phase, the Ship expels the Pirate from himself and starts attacking us with a laser

In Sally Stageplay, a woman in a theater attacks us using her umbrella and jumping on our character; In the next phases the scenario changes, as in the second where she continues attacking while babies appear attacking us in the background; in the third she becomes a cardboard character and in the attacks with 3 different attacks; and finally she appears tied to a rope while the audience throws flowers that harm us

As the last boss on this island, we have the Phantom Express. We battle against 4 horror characters with train theme, with a ghost that shoots out your eyes in the first phase; a skeleton driver in second; two brothers who attack us by shooting lightning from their mouths; and finally the train itself attacks us and We gotta get your heart right
