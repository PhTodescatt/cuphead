Curiosities

King Dice is the first boss on this island, he appears at the beginning, giving us a dice that allows us to parry, and The number that lands on his die is the secondary roulette boss that we will face. After we defeat these secondary bosses, the fight against him is unlocked and he attacks us with cards, having a single attack

The first 3 bosses we manage to face are a trio of drinks in a bar, a tower of chips and a cigarette

Soon after, we can face a domino on a swing, a magical rabbit and a skeleton horse

The last three that appear are a dancing roulette wheel, an 8 ball and a monkey that forces us to play a memory game

As the last and main boss of the game, The Devil appears, and if we agree to fight against him we will have to deal with very varied attacks. In its first phase, it shoots several types of magic and can even become a spider; as a second phase it appears giant in the background while attacks us with a flying ax and bombs; thirdly, two large demons appear spitting on the player and a few other minors who attack in the air; when we defeat these minor characters, the Devil starts crying in the background and his tears cause damage, being a phase in which he has little life. By going through all this, we beat the Devil and ended the game

Nthe end the book presents us with the true and happy ending of the story, where we see with more details at: History
